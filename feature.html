Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our features

How does the AI recommendation system work?

Our AI analyzes your reading history, ratings, time spent on different genres, and even your reading speed to understand your preferences. It then compares your profile with millions of other readers to find books with similar appeal that you haven't discovered yet.

Can I read offline?

Yes! You can download books to your device for offline reading. Your progress, bookmarks, and annotations will sync automatically once you're back online.

How many devices can I use simultaneously?

You can read on up to 5 devices simultaneously with one account. Your reading progress syncs across all devices in real-time.

Are there any reading accessibility features?

Yes, we offer comprehensive accessibility features including screen reader compatibility, dyslexia-friendly fonts, high contrast modes, adjustable text sizing and spacing, and text-to-speech functionality with multiple voice options.

Can I share books with family members?

Yes, our Family Sharing feature allows you to share your library with up to 5 family members while maintaining separate reading progress and recommendations for each person.
